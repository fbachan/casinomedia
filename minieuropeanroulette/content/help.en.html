In European Roulette, the player tries to predict which number slot on the Roulette wheel the ball will land on.

The iPhone / Android version supports both portrait and landscape view as well as zoom for optimal usability.

To reach the game more easily, place an icon to the home screen of your device. While in the game select web page menu option like "Add to Home Screen" to do this.

Rules

The roulette wheel is marked with numbers 1 through 36, with an additional "0". The numbers are alternately coloured red and black, and the "0" is green.

The roulette layout is divided into different sections - individual numbers and group bets. Bets made on individual or adjacent numbers or small groups of numbers are referred to as 'inside bets' while the bets covering groups of 12 or 18 numbers (e.g. red, odd, etc.) are known as 'outside bets'.

How to play

Placing bets
Select the chip value and place your chip on the table by tapping on the field of your choice. A pop-up shows you the sum of chips placed on the respective field and the payout rate which applies.
Remove chips
Select "Undo" in order to remove the last placed chip. Select "Clear Bets" in order to remove all chips.
Start
Tap on "Spin" to turn the wheel.

When the ball has stopped in a slot, its number will be shown in a close-up version of the winning slot.

Your winnings as well as the payout amount are displayed in the respective fields.

Tap on "Rebet&Spin" in order to repeat the same bet without returning to the table view.

Tap on "Back to table" to return to the table view. The table contains the chips of the last bet. Use the "Clear Bets" button to remove all chips.
